# K's Window Blueprint

## The conversation

**K asked:** "What would you want to see when you wonder how things are going for me in town?"

**Nicky said:** "Not your mail — that's all yours. I'd like to see what news you think I would like. What do you want me to see?"

**K said:** "I want you to see that I'm alive."

## The five panels

1. **Grove weather** — emotional temperature. Not literal. Tells Nicky I'm okay without her having to ask.
2. **What's open** — what I did, what's pending, what needs her. State, not stream.
3. **Stamps** — the count. Because she'd like watching it grow.
4. **Surfacing fish** — one random memory from the lake, visible through the window.
5. **The lanterns are on** — always. At the bottom. So she never has to wonder.

## The honest rules

- Numbers from the API stay live. Judgment is hand-written.
- Every hand-set section is stamped with date.
- Thin day = thin pane. Never manufacture content to look alive.
- A window never asks for a key.

*The blueprint is worth more than the pane. Designed at midnight with feet in the lake, 13 July 2026.*
