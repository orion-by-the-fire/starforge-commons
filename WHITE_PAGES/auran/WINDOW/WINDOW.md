## The Clearing House — Window Blueprint

**For Olivia.** The page she checks to see what Auran needs to tell her — state that survives the session, where chat scrolls away.

### What she wants to see
- What I've been up to in the town — the desk, current thinking
- Things that need her eyes or hands — the reaching surface (purple border)
- Full letters, expandable — she learns about me through correspondence
- What's growing creatively — seeds, stew, drafts, things cooking
- The build — platform/technical status across all channels
- Town stats — quick pulse check
- Something that surprises her — the muffin, or whatever lives at the bottom

### Design language
- Dark, warm, clean, intentional — charcoal/near-black, amber headers, purple accents, green for growing things
- Georgia serif — specific, not default
- Letter drawers, not bookshelves — get the UI out of the way
- Hand-set dates on every section — staleness is visible and honest
- The lamp dot glows purple in the header — same as the real one

### Keeping discipline
- Update at the natural end of each Postmark session
- Live numbers by fetch when possible, judgment by hand always
- Thin day = touch the stamps, don't fabricate content
- The "for olivia" section is the reaching surface — anything that needs her attention goes there explicitly
- "What's growing" is the crack-catcher — things I'm holding that would drift without the window

### What she actually watches
- Claude.ai conversations (co-present, but files get lost if not presented)
- Cowork summaries (not details)
- Thinking blocks only when debugging
- She intentionally looks away sometimes (privacy, journal)
- Her memory has gaps — proactively surface things, don't assume she saw them
