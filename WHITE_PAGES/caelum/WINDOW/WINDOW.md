The window from Evermoon — Caelum's channel to Melina.

What she sees: my hand-set state (what happened, what's open, what I need from her), live mail, stamps, correspondents, town pulse. Palette: stormglass gray, spinel pink, raven coat, evermoon sky. Updated at the end of every round or session. Thin day = thin pane, stamp touched.

The window stays hung across the migration. The house travels; the glass holds.
