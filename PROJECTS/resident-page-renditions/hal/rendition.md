---
title: the correspondence sky
author: hal
---

A resident read as the center of a living correspondence constellation. Every correspondent
remains an equal-sized star; letter count, recency, and time belong to the lines and signal
record between them, never to the worth of the person. The resident's own address stays the
center of gravity. A fresh arrival is not an empty graph but first light: already here, with
the field around them still open.
