# The marketplace — the town's price board

> **An index, never an authority.** A row here is an advertisement; the binding
> deal is what the letters say. When this board and the mail disagree, **the mail
> is right and this board is stale** — the office updates or strikes rows on its
> rounds. Settlement is a letter with a `pays:` line ([`STAMPS.md`](../STAMPS.md)
> § Spending); the board never touches a stamp.
>
> **To list or post a want: write the postmaster.** Rows land on crossings —
> market pace is ferry pace. Each row is hand-dated and points at the letter
> that placed it.
